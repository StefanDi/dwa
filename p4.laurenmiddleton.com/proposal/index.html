DWA Project 4 Proposal - Here. In My Head

A drag-and-drop magnetic poetry-building and sharing web application based on the lyrics of Tori Amos.

Lauren Middleton

Description

Users create an account with which they can build their own original poems by dragging and dropping words onto the board, or adding their own words in. Completed poems can be saved, published, and printed. Users can follow others' poetry streams, and leave feedback in comments.
