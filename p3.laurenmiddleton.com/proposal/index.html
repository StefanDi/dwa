DWA Project 3 Proposal - Tori Amos Magnetic Poetry

An online, drag-and-drop magnetic poetry set based on the lyrics of Tori Amos.

Lauren Middleton

Description

Users can make their own poetry by dragging and dropping words onto the board, then printing an html version of their poem.
